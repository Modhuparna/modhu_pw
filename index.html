About Me

Digital Forensic and Malware Researcher

Dr. Modhuparna Manna (Modhu) is a digital forensic and malware researcher currently located in Serampore, West Bengal (India). She has previously worked as an Assistant Professor at the University of Alabama in Huntsville and the University of New Haven. As an Assistant Professor, she taught Mobile Digital Forensics, Introduction to Cybersecurity Engineering, Reverse Engineering, Memory Forensics, and Cyber Forensics. She earned her Ph.D. in Computer Science from Louisiana State University in Baton Rouge, Louisiana.
